Step 2: Supercharge Your Experience with Our Extension

For the full Resource Saver Pro experience, let's get our extension set up. Don't worry, it's quick and easy!

  1. Download the extension ZIP file
  2. Unzip the file to a folder on your computer
  3. Open Google Chrome and go to chrome://extensions
  4. Enable "Developer mode" in the top right corner
  5. Click "Load unpacked" and select the folder where you unzipped the extension
  6. You're all set! You should see the Resource Saver Pro icon in your Chrome toolbar

Step 3: Start Saving!

Now you're ready to start curating your digital world. Here are some things you can do:

  • Click the extension icon to save any webpage you're on

  • Add notes and tags to your saved resources for easy retrieval
